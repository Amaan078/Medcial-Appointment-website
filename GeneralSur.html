Dr.Vivek Pratap Singh

Profile Summary

Dr. Vivek Pratap Singh is an Enthusiastic surgeon with variegated surgical expertise in General, Trauma, and Minimal access procedures. He earned his MBBS from PGIMS, Rohtak, and won two Gold Medals in different categories in his MS at JNMC, Belgaum. Thereafter he pursued various courses and fellowships to sharpen his skills. He has delivered presentations at National level forums and published research papers and case studies too. Prior to joining SPS Hospitals, he has been attached to prestigious institutions in Bangalore and Delhi Like Bhagwan Mahavir Jain Hospital and Sanjay Gandhi Memorial Hospital, and further got trained in UGI endoscopy, Laser, and Minimal access GI procedures. He also strives for an Entrepreneurial model to deliver quality-based, Equitable Surgical healthcare solutions as in to cater masses in Rural India.
